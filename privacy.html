Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Introduction

Deva Decor ("we," "us," or "our") operates devadecor.com (the "Site"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Site.

Our Store

Deva Decor curates and sells premium home decor through our online store. When you purchase a product, you will be directed to our storefront on Amazon India (amazon.in) where the transaction is completed securely. Product prices and availability are subject to change.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information:

  • Newsletter Subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address solely for the purpose of sending you curated decor picks and deals.
  • Contact Form: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email, and message to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and browser type to improve our Site experience.

Cookies and Tracking

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Provide basic site analytics
  • Support order tracking and referral links

Third-party services, including Amazon India (amazon.in), may set their own cookies when you visit external sites through our links. These cookies are governed by those sites' own privacy policies.

Third-Party Links

Our Site contains links to Amazon India (amazon.in) and other third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the personal information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link provided in each email
  • Request deletion of any personal data we hold about you
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
