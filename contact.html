Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

All our products are sold exclusively through Amazon India. Click "Check Price on Amazon" on any product to view pricing and place your order directly on Amazon.

What is the return policy?

Returns are handled by Amazon India as per their standard return policy. Most items can be returned within 7–10 days of delivery. Check the product page on Amazon for specific return eligibility.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery is fulfilled by Amazon India. Standard delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Prime-eligible items may arrive faster.

How do I track my order?

Once you place an order on Amazon, you can track it through your Amazon account under "Your Orders".

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

Yes, we offer bulk pricing for businesses and interior designers. Email us at hello@devadecor.com with your requirements.
